Beyond Academics

School Events & Activities

Eco Club

Go Green Campaign

Under our school Eco Club, students regularly conduct awareness rallies in local villages to promote plastic reduction, waste segregation, and tree conservation.

Annual Event

World Environment Day Drive

Every year on June 5th, GIC Gorangchaur organizes an extensive tree plantation campaign. Students plant hundreds of native tree saplings across nearby mountain borders.

Civics Club

Civics & Social Awareness

Workshops and debate meets organized to educate senior students about democratic rights, constitution duties, road safety precautions, and community hygiene standards.

Cultural

Folk Arts & Cultural Meets

Promoting traditional Kumaoni music, folk dances (Choliya, Jhora), and local drama arts to keep students connected to their rich Himalayan heritage.
