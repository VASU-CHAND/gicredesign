Our School Facilities

Computer Lab (ICT)

Equipped with working desktop computers, networking resources, and internet connectivity. It provides student training in basic programming, office tools, and internet research.

Science Laboratories

Fully functional labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Students conduct practical experiments under teacher guidance to deepen scientific concepts.

School Library

Features a spacious reading area with a collection of academic reference guides, journals, literature, and news journals to promote reading habits.

Sports Playground

An expansive outdoor field providing setup for volleyball, running events, football, and team building exercises. Helps maintain physical fitness and spirit.

Mid-Day Meal Program

A hygienic and nutrition-rich meal provided daily in compliance with state regulations, ensuring students receive balanced dietary requirements during school hours.
