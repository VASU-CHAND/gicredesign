About Our School

Dedicated to Educational Excellence

Government Inter College Gorangchaur is a premier public educational institution situated amidst the scenic hills of Pithoragarh, Uttarakhand.

We are committed to providing top-quality education while nurturing strong moral values, discipline, physical health, and community responsibility in every student.

With an emphasis on traditional values mixed with scientific understanding, our students consistently excel in state board examinations, sports, and state-level cultural meets.
