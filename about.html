About Our Institution

Brief History

School History & Legacy

Serving the children of Kumaon region with pride and quality education.

Government Inter College Gorangchaur was established in Pithoragarh, Uttarakhand, with the noble goal of bringing accessible education to children in remote hilly regions. Over the decades, the institution has grown from a humble school into a prestigious Intermediate College, catering to hundreds of students from surrounding villages.

With every passing year, GIC Gorangchaur has expanded its facilities, establishing advanced computer labs (ICT), science laboratories, a well-stocked library, and sports training. Today, we are proud to offer Science, Commerce, and Arts streams, preparing students for board exams and competitive future careers.

🌟

Our Vision

To cultivate an inclusive and inspiring environment that empowers students from all backgrounds to reach their full potential. We envision our graduates as morally upright, intellectually strong, and socially responsible citizens who lead with wisdom and compassion.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide qualitative value-based education that matches modern technological changes while preserving regional culture and morals. We are committed to developing critical thinking, scientific curiosity, physical fitness, and civic awareness in students through structural academics and co-curricular programs.
