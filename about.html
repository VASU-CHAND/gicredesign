About Government Inter College Gorangchaur

Empowering young minds through quality education, discipline, values and holistic development.

Our School

Government Inter College Gorangchaur is one of the leading educational institutions in Pithoragarh, Uttarakhand. The school is dedicated to providing quality education while nurturing discipline, leadership, creativity and strong moral values.

Our mission is to create a positive learning environment where students can develop academically, socially and emotionally to become responsible citizens of the future.
