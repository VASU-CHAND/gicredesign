How to Apply for Admissions

Step-by-Step Registration

Admissions at GIC Gorangchaur are open for classes 6th to 11th. We offer Science, Commerce, and Arts streams for senior secondary classes (11th & 12th).

  • 1

    Collect Application Form

    Parents/Guardians can obtain the registration form from the administrative desk between 9:30 AM and 1:30 PM on working days.

  • 2

    Submit Required Credentials

    Complete the application form and attach necessary academic transcripts, transfer certificates, and proof of residence.

  • 3

    Verification & Fees Deposit

    After verification of credentials, deposit the admission fees at the accounts counter to secure the student's enrollment.

Required Document Checklist

  • ✔ Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous recognized school.
  • ✔ Original Progress Report Card / Marksheet of the last class passed.
  • ✔ Passport size color photographs of the student (4 copies).
  • ✔ Copy of Student's Aadhar Card.
  • ✔ Copy of Parent's ID/Address Proof (Aadhar, Voter Card).
  • ✔ Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC) if applicable, for government database.
